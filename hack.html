    What are game-based assessments


    Gamified assessment refers to the use of game design elements and mechanics, such as points, badges, levels, and challenges, in assessment processes to increase engagement, motivation, and learning outcomes. It can be applied to various types of assessments, such as skill tests, knowledge quizzes, personality assessments, and job simulations.
    In gamified assessment, the assessment tasks are designed to resemble games, with clear goals, feedback, and progress indicators. The assessment takers are incentivized to complete the tasks by earning rewards, advancing to higher levels, and competing with others.
